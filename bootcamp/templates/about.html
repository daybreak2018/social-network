ABOUT PRITILATA CHHATRINIBAS

Established in 2000, Pritilata Chhatrinibas, is the only girls' hostel of Kalyani Government Engineering College. Surrounded by lush green on all sides, it is an abode to hard-working individuals with budding talents.

It consists of two wings- the old building which consists of approximately 40 rooms and the new building consisting of approximately 10 rooms for freshers. All together it houses above 130 students per year. The hostel is well secured with two guards on duty all day round.

The environment is a progressive one which provides all kind of facilities a college student requires. At the same time, provides stage for nurturing the extra-curricular interests of the hosteliers.
